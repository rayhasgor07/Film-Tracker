# 🎬 Film Tracker  
A beautifully designed, lightweight movie discovery and tracking app powered by **The Movie Database (TMDB)** API.

Film Tracker lets you browse real Bollywood & Hollywood films, track what you've watched, save favourites, rate movies, write personal notes, and filter your entire library — all stored locally in your browser.

No backend. No accounts. No databases.  
Just a clean, fast, privacy‑friendly movie tracker.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔍 **Movie Discovery**
- Fetches real films directly from TMDB  
- Bollywood (Hindi) and Hollywood (English) toggle  
- Infinite scrolling with “Load More”  
- Smart search with live filtering  

### 🎛 **Powerful Filters**
- Genre pills (Action, Comedy, Drama, etc.)  
- Era filters (90s, 2000s, 2010s, 2020s)  
- Minimum TMDB rating slider  
- Sort by:
  - Newest
  - Oldest
  - Rating
  - Alphabetical
  - Popularity

### 🎞 **Movie Details Modal**
- High‑quality poster & backdrop  
- Year, rating, vote count, genres  
- Full overview  
- Your personal actions:
  - Mark as **Watched**
  - Add to **Favourites**
  - ⭐ Rate (1–5 stars)
  - 📝 Write notes

### 🧠 **Local Storage Sync**
All your data is saved automatically:
- Watched list  
- Favourites  
- Ratings  
- Notes  
- Filters & tab state  

No login. No cloud. Everything stays on your device.
